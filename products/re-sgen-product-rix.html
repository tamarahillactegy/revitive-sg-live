REVITIVE IX

Good health and an active lifestyle require good circulation. If you are one of the thousands of people who experience poor circulation resulting in swollen ankles, cold feet or aching legs, then Revitive Circulation Booster may be the health tool for you!

Revitive IX Class B medical device designed to improve blood circulation in the lower legs and feet. It uses Electrical Muscle Stimulation (EMS) to stimulate the muscles in your legs and feet through two large footpads. The muscle pumping action immediately increases blood flow, thereby providing fast acting relief from aches, pains and swelling.

REVITIVE - Proven to do exactly what it says…

  • Increase lower leg circulation

REVITIVE IX Features

IsoRocker

The IsoRocker allows the REVITIVE IX to gently ‘rock’ back and forth allowing natural ankle movement to replicate heel toe raises.

WidePulse waveforms

WidePulse waveforms are our latest breakthrough in circulation technology which results in a more comfortable feeling stimulation.

Remote Control

For ease of use the Revitive IX comes with a remote control and batteries are included.

TENS pads

To target specific areas of pain the Revitive IX includes 4 T.E.N.S pads (2 pairs). Simply plug the lead into your device, place the T.E.N.S. pads on the area you want to target (e.g. shoulders, lower back, knee, arm etc) and use the remote control or buttons on the device to increase the intensity to a comfortable level. Can be used at the same time as using your Revitive IX on your feet or separately as required. Always read the manual and only use as intended.
